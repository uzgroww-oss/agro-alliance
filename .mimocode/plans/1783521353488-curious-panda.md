## Yechim
Groq dan **batafsil rasm tavsifi** olish (1-2 jumlada, ingliz tilida) va uni to'g'ridan-to'g'ri Pollinations prompt sifatida ishlatish.

### Misol:
- Hozir: `img: ["agriculture", "farming"]` → random agro rasm
- Yangi: `img_prompt: "A modern precision agriculture drone spraying crops in a golden wheat field at sunset, photorealistic"` → maqolaga mos rasm

## Fayllar:
- `supabase/functions/worker-ai-news-engine/index.ts` — Groq promptini yangilash
  - `img: string[]` → `img_prompt: string` ga o'zgartirish
  - Groq promptiga: "img_prompt: 1-2 sentence detailed English description of what the image should show, photorealistic style"
  - URL: `https://image.pollinations.ai/prompt/${encodeURIComponent(img_prompt)}?width=800&height=500&nologo=true`

## Tekshirish:
1. AI engine trigger qilish
2. Yangi maqolalarning cover_image URL ni tekshirish
